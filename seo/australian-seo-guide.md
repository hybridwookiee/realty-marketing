This guide is specifically tailored for Australian real estate businesses, with a focus on Gippsland, Victoria. It covers Australian platforms, Google Business Profile optimisation, and local SEO strategies.

## 1. Google Business Profile (Australian Optimisation)

### Setup Steps:
1. Go to [google.com/business](https://www.google.com/business)
2. Create or claim your business profile
3. Complete ALL sections with Australian-specific information

### Critical Information:
- **Business Name**: @realty Property Sales Gippsland (or Gippsland Property Sales)
- **Address**: Your Gippsland office address (must be accurate)
- **Phone**: Australian format (e.g., +61 3 XXXX XXXX or 03 XXXX XXXX)
- **Category**: "Real Estate Agency" or "Real Estate Agent"
- **Service Areas**: 
  - Drouin
  - Trafalgar
  - Warragul
  - Moe
  - Traralgon
  - Gippsland

### Australian-Specific Optimisation:
- **Business Hours**: Include AEST timezone
- **Photos**: Add 20+ high-quality photos (properties, team, office)
- **Services**: List all services in Australian English
- **Description**: Include keywords like:
  - "real estate agent Gippsland"
  - "property sales Drouin"
  - "sell house Trafalgar"
  - "Gippsland property appraisal"

### Review Strategy:
- **Get Reviews**: Aim for 10+ Google reviews
- **Respond to ALL Reviews**: Both positive and negative
- **Review Request Process**:
  1. After every transaction, send follow-up email
  2. Include direct link to Google review page
  3. Make it easy: "Click here to leave a review"
  4. Follow up if no review after 1 week

### Google Posts (Australian Content):
- Post 2-3 times per week
- Content ideas:
  - New listings in Drouin, Trafalgar, Warragul
  - Gippsland market updates
  - Property tips
  - Local area highlights
  - Open house announcements
