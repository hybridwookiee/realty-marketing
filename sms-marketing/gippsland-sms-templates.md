# Gippsland Property Sales - SMS Marketing Templates

## Overview
These SMS templates are specifically customised for @realty Property Sales Gippsland, focusing on property appraisals, new listings, and lead nurturing. All messages use Australian English and Gippsland-specific content.

**Important**: All SMS must include:
- Business name: "@realty Property Sales Gippsland"
- Opt-out: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
- Keep under 160 characters (one SMS) when possible

---

## Best Practices for SMS

### Message Structure:
1. **Greeting**: "Hi [Name]!" or just "[Name]!"
2. **Value**: What's in it for them?
3. **Action**: What should they do?
4. **Compliance**: Opt-out + business name

### Timing Guidelines:
- **Property Alerts**: Can send anytime (high value)
- **Appointments**: 24 hours before
- **Follow-ups**: 2-4 hours after interaction
- **Updates**: Weekday mornings (9am-12pm)
- **Avoid**: Before 8am, after 8pm, weekends (unless urgent)

### Frequency:
- **Maximum**: 2-4 SMS per month
- **Property Alerts**: Unlimited (they opted in for this)
- **Nurture Sequence**: 1-2 per week maximum
- **Transactional**: As needed (appointments, offers, etc.)

### Character Limits:
- **Single SMS**: 160 characters (standard)
- **Long Messages**: Will split into multiple SMS (costs more)
- **Best Practice**: Keep under 160 when possible
- **URLs**: Always use short links (bit.ly, tinyurl.com)
