# Domain.com.au Setup Guide

## Overview
Domain.com.au is the second-largest property website in Australia. Having an optimised agent profile here complements your RealEstate.com.au presence and increases your visibility.

---

## Why Domain.com.au Matters

- **Second-largest property site** in Australia
- **Different audience** than RealEstate.com.au
- **Additional lead source** for your business
- **Market insights** and analytics
- **Agent reviews** build credibility

---

## Step 1: Create/Claim Your Agent Profile

### Access:
1. Go to [domain.com.au](https://www.domain.com.au)
2. Look for "Agents" or "For Agents" section
3. Click "Sign Up" or "Become an Agent"
4. If you already have listings, your profile may already exist

### Registration:
- Use your business email
- Create password
- Verify email address
- Complete registration

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

### Basic Information:
- **Name**: Your name or business name (@realty Property Sales Gippsland)
- **Phone**: Australian format (+61 or 03)
- **Email**: Business email
- **Office Address**: Your Gippsland office address
- **Service Areas**: 
  - Drouin
  - Trafalgar
  - Warragul
  - Moe
  - Traralgon
  - Gippsland

### Professional Photo:
- **Requirements**: High-quality, professional headshot
- **Size**: At least 400x400 pixels
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Tips**: 
  - Professional attire
  - Good lighting
  - Clear background
  - Friendly expression

### Bio/Description:
**Include:**
- Your experience in Gippsland
- Specialisations (property appraisals, sales, etc.)
- Service areas (Drouin, Trafalgar, Warragul, etc.)
- What makes you different
- @realty network credentials

**Example:**
```
Expert real estate agent specialising in Gippsland property sales and 
appraisals. With deep local knowledge of Drouin, Trafalgar, Warragul, 
Moe, Traralgon and surrounding Gippsland areas.

As part of the @realty network, I've helped clients buy and sell 
properties across Gippsland, providing expert local market knowledge 
and personalised service.

Specialising in:
- Free property appraisals (no obligation)
- Residential property sales
- Local Gippsland market expertise
- Professional marketing and negotiation

Whether you're buying or selling in Gippsland, I'm here to help you 
achieve your property goals.
```

**Keywords to Include:**
- Gippsland
- Drouin
- Trafalgar
- Warragul
- Property sales
- Property appraisal
- Real estate agent

## Step 3: Add Your Listings

### Link Existing Listings:
- If you already have listings on Domain.com.au, link them to your profile
- This builds your portfolio and credibility

### Create New Listings:
- When you list a property, ensure it's linked to your profile
- Use high-quality photos (20+ photos recommended)
- Write detailed descriptions
- Include local area keywords

---

## Step 4: Optimise for Lead Generation

### Contact Information:
- **Phone**: Make it clickable (mobile users can call directly)
- **Email**: Professional email address
- **Office**: Include office address and hours

### Call-to-Action:
- Encourage visitors to contact you
- Mention free property appraisals
- Highlight your Gippsland expertise

---

## Step 5: Get Reviews

### Why Reviews Matter:
- Builds trust and credibility
- Improves your profile ranking
- Shows social proof
- Helps generate leads

### How to Get Reviews:
1. **After Every Transaction:**
   - Send follow-up email
   - Include direct link to review page
   - Make it easy: "Click here to leave a review"

2. **Review Request Email Template:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Thank you for choosing @realty Property Sales Gippsland for your 
property journey. We hope you had a great experience!

If you have a moment, we'd love it if you could leave us a review 
on Domain.com.au. Your feedback helps other Gippsland homeowners 
find the right real estate agent.

[Direct Link to Review Page]

Thank you so much!

[Your Name]
@realty Property Sales Gippsland
```

3. **Follow Up:**
   - If no review after 1 week, send gentle reminder
   - Don't be pushy, but don't be afraid to ask

### Respond to Reviews:
- **Respond to ALL reviews** (positive and negative)
- Thank positive reviewers
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Shows you care about client experience

## Step 7: Content & Engagement

## Step 8: Lead Management

### Respond Quickly:
- **Target**: Respond within 1 hour
- **Why**: Fast response = higher conversion
- **How**: Set up email notifications
- **Mobile**: Use Domain.com.au app for quick responses

### Qualification:
- Ask qualifying questions
- Understand their timeline
- Assess their motivation
- Schedule property viewings or appraisals

### Follow-Up:
- Don't let leads go cold
- Follow up within 24 hours if no response
- Use email sequence for nurturing
