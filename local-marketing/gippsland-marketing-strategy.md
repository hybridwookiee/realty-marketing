# Gippsland Marketing Strategy

## Overview
This guide provides Gippsland-specific marketing content ideas, local area strategies, and community engagement tactics for @realty Property Sales Gippsland.

---

## Gippsland Market Overview

### Key Suburbs:
- **Drouin**: Primary service area, strong property market
- **Trafalgar**: Growing area, good investment potential
- **Warragul**: Regional hub, diverse property types
- **Moe**: Affordable options, first-home buyer friendly
- **Traralgon**: Larger town, commercial and residential

### Market Characteristics:
- Mix of residential, rural, and commercial properties
- Price range: $350,000 - $1,500,000+
- Strong local community
- Growing population
- Good infrastructure and amenities
