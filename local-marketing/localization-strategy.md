# Localization Strategy: Attracting Local Customers & Reducing Non-Local Followers

## The Problem

You're getting followers and engagement from people outside your service area (Gippsland: Warragul, Drouin, Yarragon, Trafalgar). This dilutes your marketing efforts and wastes ad spend on people who can't use your services.

**Current Issues:**
- 7,000+ fake followers from outside Australia (China, Pakistan, India)
- Organic followers from outside Gippsland
- Ads reaching people who can't buy/sell in your area
- Low engagement from non-local followers affecting algorithm

---

## Strategy Overview

**Three-Pronged Approach:**
1. **Prevent** non-local followers from finding you
2. **Target** only local audiences in ads and content
3. **Engage** with local community to build authentic local following

---

## 6. Reducing Non-Local Followers

### Active Cleanup

**Daily Routine (10-15 minutes):**
1. Go to Facebook Business Suite ‚Üí **Insights** ‚Üí **Followers**
2. Review followers for:
   - No location in Australia
   - Location outside Victoria/Gippsland
   - No profile picture + foreign name
   - Zero engagement with your posts
3. **Remove followers** who are clearly non-local
4. Target: Remove 50-100 non-local followers per day

**Priority Removal:**
1. **International followers** (China, Pakistan, India, etc.)
2. **Other Australian states** (NSW, QLD, etc. if not serving)
3. **Melbourne/Sydney** (if outside service area)
4. **Fake accounts** (no photo, no posts, no friends)

### Prevention

**Settings to Prevent Non-Local Followers:**

1. **Facebook Page Settings:**
   - **Page Moderation**: Block certain words (optional)
   - **Country Restrictions**: Can't restrict by country, but use content strategy

2. **Ad Targeting:**
   - **Always exclude** non-service areas in ads
   - Use **lookalike audiences** based on local customers only
   - **Don't boost posts** without location targeting

3. **Content Strategy:**
   - Post **hyper-local content** that non-locals won't engage with
   - Use **local hashtags** only
   - **Don't use viral/broad hashtags** that attract non-locals

## 11. Advanced Strategies

### Lookalike Audiences (Local Only)

**Create Local Lookalike:**
1. Upload list of **local customers only** (from your CRM)
2. Create **Custom Audience** from this list
3. Create **Lookalike Audience** (1-2% similarity)
4. **Restrict lookalike to Gippsland/Victoria only**
5. Use for ad targeting

**Why This Works:**
- Facebook finds people similar to your local customers
- Restricted to your service area
- Higher quality, more local leads

### Retargeting (Local Visitors Only)

**Website Retargeting:**
1. Install Facebook Pixel
2. Create **Custom Audience**: Website visitors (last 30 days)
3. **Layer location targeting**: Gippsland only
4. Show ads only to local website visitors

**Why This Works:**
- People who visited your site are interested
- Location layer ensures they're local
- Higher conversion rate
