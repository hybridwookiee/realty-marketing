## Form Submission Setup Options

### Option 1: Google Forms (Easiest - FREE)
1. Create Google Form matching your landing page fields
2. Get embed code
3. Replace form on landing page with Google Form embed
4. Responses automatically go to Google Sheet

### Option 2: EmailJS (FREE - 200 emails/month)
1. Sign up at [emailjs.com](https://www.emailjs.com)
2. Create email template
3. Add EmailJS script to your landing page
4. Form submissions send email directly to you

### Option 3: Formspree (FREE - 50 submissions/month)
1. Sign up at [formspree.io](https://formspree.io)
2. Get form endpoint URL
3. Update form action to Formspree URL
4. Submissions sent to your email

### Option 4: Zapier + Google Sheets (FREE tier)
1. Connect your form to Zapier
2. Zapier sends to Google Sheets
3. Also triggers email automation

## Troubleshooting

### "I'm not getting any leads"
- Check: Are your ads running? (Check ad status)
- Check: Are your landing pages working? (Test form)
- Check: Is your targeting too narrow? (Expand audience)
- Check: Are your ads showing? (Check impressions)

### "Leads are too expensive"
- Improve landing page (better conversion rate = lower cost per lead)
- Refine targeting (more specific = better quality)
- Test new ad copy (better CTR = lower CPC)
- Optimize keywords (remove low-performing)

### "Leads aren't converting to sales"
- Follow up faster (within 5 minutes for hot leads)
- Improve qualification process
- Nurture leads longer (email sequence)
- Ask for referrals from past clients
