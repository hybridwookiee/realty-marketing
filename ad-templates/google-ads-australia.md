## Tracking & Conversion Setup

### Set Up Conversions:
1. **Form Submissions**: Track when someone fills out your appraisal form
2. **Phone Calls**: Track calls from your ads (use call tracking number)
3. **Email Signups**: Track newsletter signups
4. **Property Views**: Track when someone views a property listing

### Google Ads Conversion Tracking Code:
Add to your landing page's `<head>`:
```html
<!-- Google tag (gtag.js) -->
<script async src="https://www.googletagmanager.com/gtag/js?id=AW-CONVERSION_ID"></script>
<script>
  window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
  function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
  gtag('js', new Date());
  gtag('config', 'AW-CONVERSION_ID');
</script>
```
