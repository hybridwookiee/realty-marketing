## Ad Format Best Practices

### Image Ads:
- **Size**: 1200 x 628 pixels (1.91:1 ratio)
- **Text**: Keep text on image minimal (Facebook prefers <20% text)
- **Quality**: High-resolution, professional photos
- **Branding**: Include your logo subtly

### Video Ads:
- **Length**: 15-60 seconds (30 seconds is sweet spot)
- **Format**: Square (1:1) or Landscape (16:9)
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds are critical - grab attention!
- **Captions**: Always add captions (85% watch without sound)
- **CTA**: Include call-to-action in video

### Carousel Ads:
- **Cards**: 3-10 cards (5-7 is optimal)
- **Images**: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square)
- **Consistency**: Keep design/style consistent across cards
- **Story**: Tell a story as user swipes through

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Test These Elements:
1. **Ad Copy**: Long vs. short, emotional vs. factual
2. **Images**: Professional photos vs. lifestyle images
3. **Headlines**: Question vs. statement
4. **Call-to-Action**: "Learn More" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Contact Us"
5. **Audiences**: Lookalike 1% vs. 2% vs. Interest-based
6. **Placements**: Feed vs. Stories vs. Messenger

### Testing Best Practices:
- Test ONE variable at a time
- Run tests for at least 7 days (preferably 14)
- Use statistical significance (Facebook will show this)
- Keep winners, pause losers

## Pro Tips

1. **Start with Retargeting**: Cheapest, highest converting audience
2. **Use Video**: Video ads typically perform 2-3x better than images
3. **Test Constantly**: Facebook's algorithm changes, keep testing
4. **Focus on Mobile**: 90%+ of Facebook users are on mobile
5. **Engage Comments**: Respond to every comment quickly
6. **Use Stories**: Facebook Stories ads often have lower CPC
7. **Seasonal Timing**: Real estate is seasonal - adjust budgets accordingly
