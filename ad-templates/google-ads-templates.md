# Google Ads Templates for Real Estate

## Overview
These are ready-to-use Google Ads templates that you can copy and paste directly into Google Ads. No $18,000 needed!


## Campaign Settings

### Budget Recommendations:
- **Starting Budget**: $500-1,000/month
- **Daily Budget**: $20-35/day
- **Bid Strategy**: Maximize Conversions (after 30 conversions) or Target CPA

### Targeting:
- **Location**: Your city + surrounding areas (10-20 mile radius)
- **Demographics**: All (unless you have specific target market)
- **Devices**: All devices (mobile is critical for real estate)

## Landing Page Requirements

**Critical**: Your ads MUST link to relevant landing pages, not just your homepage!

### Home Buyers Landing Page:
- Clear headline: "Find Your Dream Home in [City]"
- Lead capture form (name, email, phone, property type, budget)
- Benefits/features
- Social proof (testimonials, recent sales)
- Call-to-action buttons

### Home Sellers Landing Page:
- Clear headline: "Sell Your Home for Top Dollar"
- Home valuation form
- Benefits of working with you
- Recent sales data
- Call-to-action buttons

## Tracking & Conversion Setup

### Set Up Conversions:
1. **Form Submissions**: Track when someone fills out your contact form
2. **Phone Calls**: Track calls from your ads (use call tracking number)
3. **Email Signups**: Track newsletter signups
4. **Property Views**: Track when someone views a property listing

### Google Ads Conversion Tracking Code:
Add to your website's `<head>`:
```html
<!-- Google tag (gtag.js) -->
<script async src="https://www.googletagmanager.com/gtag/js?id=AW-CONVERSION_ID"></script>
<script>
  window.dataLayer = window.dataLayer || [];
  function gtag(){dataLayer.push(arguments);}
  gtag('js', new Date());
  gtag('config', 'AW-CONVERSION_ID');
</script>
```

## Optimization Tips

### Week 1-2:
- Launch campaigns
- Monitor daily
- Pause underperforming keywords
- Increase bids on winners

### Week 3-4:
- Review search terms report
- Add negative keywords
- Test new ad copy
- Optimize landing pages

### Month 2+:
- Scale winning campaigns
- Add new ad groups for different neighborhoods
- Test different bid strategies
- Expand to Display/YouTube if search performs well

## Expected Results

**Realistic Expectations:**
- **Cost per Click (CPC)**: $2-8 (varies by city/competition)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: 3-7% (good performance)
- **Conversion Rate**: 2-5% (form submissions/calls)
- **Cost per Lead**: $40-150 (depends on competition)

**Example:**
- Budget: $1,000/month
- Clicks: 200 (at $5 CPC)
- Conversions: 6 (at 3% conversion rate)
- Cost per Lead: $167
