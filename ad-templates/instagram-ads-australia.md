## Ad Format Best Practices

### Instagram Feed Posts:
- **Size**: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square) OR 1080 x 1350 pixels (portrait)
- **Text**: Minimal text on image - Instagram is visual-first
- **Quality**: High-resolution, professional photos (Instagram users expect high quality)
- **Branding**: Include @realty logo subtly
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) or Portrait (4:5) perform best

### Instagram Reels:
- **Length**: 15-90 seconds (30 seconds is sweet spot)
- **Format**: 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 vertical)
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds are CRITICAL - grab attention immediately!
- **Captions**: Always add captions (most watch without sound)
- **Text Overlay**: Use text overlays to reinforce key messages
- **Trending Audio**: Use trending sounds when appropriate (but keep it professional)

### Instagram Stories:
- **Size**: 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 vertical)
- **Length**: 15 seconds per slide (max 15 slides)
- **Interactive Elements**: Use polls, questions, swipe-up links
- **Text**: Large, readable text (users view on mobile)
- **Stickers**: Use location stickers, hashtag stickers (sparingly)

### Carousel Ads:
- **Cards**: 3-10 cards (5-7 is optimal)
- **Images**: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square)
- **Consistency**: Keep design/style consistent across cards
- **Story**: Tell a story as user swipes through
- **First Image**: Make it count - many users don't swipe

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Test These Elements:
1. **Ad Format**: Reels vs. Stories vs. Feed Posts
2. **Ad Copy**: Short vs. long captions
3. **Images**: Professional photos vs. lifestyle images vs. behind-the-scenes
4. **Video Length**: 15s vs. 30s vs. 60s
5. **Hashtags**: 3 hashtags vs. 5 hashtags vs. no hashtags
6. **CTA**: "Link in Bio" vs. "DM Us" vs. "Swipe Up"
7. **Audiences**: Lookalike 1% vs. 2% vs. Interest-based
8. **Placements**: Feed vs. Stories vs. Reels

### Testing Best Practices:
- Test ONE variable at a time
- Run tests for at least 7 days (preferably 14)
- Use statistical significance (Instagram will show this)
- Keep winners, pause losers
- Instagram algorithm changes frequently - keep testing

## Pro Tips

1. **Reels First**: Reels get the most reach - prioritise Reels ads
2. **Visual Quality**: Instagram users expect high-quality visuals - invest in good photos
3. **Authenticity**: Instagram users value authenticity - show behind-the-scenes, real moments
4. **Stories Ads**: Often cheaper than Feed ads - test them!
5. **Engage Quickly**: Respond to comments and DMs within 1 hour
6. **User-Generated Content**: Repost client success stories (with permission)
7. **Consistent Posting**: Post organic content 3-5x per week alongside ads
8. **Local Focus**: Always emphasise Gippsland, Drouin, Trafalgar, Warragul
9. **Video Over Image**: Video content typically performs 2-3x better
10. **Mobile-First**: 100% of Instagram users are on mobile - design for mobile

## Instagram vs Facebook Ads

| Feature | Instagram | Facebook |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Best For** | Visual content, younger audience | Detailed info, older audience |
| **Ad Formats** | Reels, Stories, Feed | Feed, Stories, Messenger |
| **Text Length** | Short captions | Longer copy OK |
| **Hashtags** | 3-5 recommended | 1-2 optional |
| **Engagement** | Higher engagement rate | Lower engagement rate |
| **CPC** | Slightly higher | Slightly lower |
| **Visual Quality** | Must be high | Can be more casual |

**Recommendation**: Run both! Use Instagram for visual property showcases and Facebook for detailed information and older demographics.
